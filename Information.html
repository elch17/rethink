Donate and Volunteer

Here are a list of charities in the UK, founded to support menstration access

  • no more taboo

    WEBSITE: nomoretaboo.org

    no more taboo promotes the normalization of periods and is dedicated to empowering women to speak about them. no more taboo provides resources to manage your periods sustainably. Thereby creating empowerment to make their own choices. “No one, wherever they live and whoever they are, should have to go without.”

    Donate to no more taboo

    Volunteer with no more taboo

  • Free Periods

    WEBSITE: freeperiods.org

    Free Periods campaign was started by Amika George after reading about children missing school as they were unable to afford menstrual products. Started as an online movement, Free Periods has since received global recognition for their campaigning work. They next phase of Free Periods is raising funds for a legal campaign to ensure every child has equal access to education.

    Donate to Free Periods

    Volunteer with Free Periods

  • The Real Period Project

    WEBSITE: realperiodproject.org/

    At The Real Period Project, they believe periods without shame, and to fully informed girls about the process of the menstrual cycle and their choices around it. They also believe it’s important to teach about periods in the context of the whole menstrual cycle.
