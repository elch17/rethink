University of Bristol Resources


The University of Bristol Health Centre website offers a number of different student services, such as counselling and crisis support. UoB also offers advice for staying healthy on campus.

UoB students can also register with an NHS GP. The health centre is located at Hampton House Health Centre, St Michael's Hill.
